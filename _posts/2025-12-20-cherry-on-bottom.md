## Ingredients

- 2 ounces whiskey, preferably Angel's Envy
- 1 ounce lemon juice
- 3/4 oz amaro, preferably Amaro di Angostura
- 1/2 oz black cherry syrup, such as Filthy Black Cherry Syrup
- 1/4 oz simple syrup

## Directions

Shaken.
