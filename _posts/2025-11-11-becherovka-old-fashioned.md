## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 ounces Becherovka
- 1/2 ounce high-proof bourbon, such as Old Forester Single Barrel
- 1/2 ounce honey syrup
- 2 dashes walnut bitters

## Directions

Stirred.

## Sources

Jack Thomas and Casey Wharton

## Comments

#### 2025-11-11 (Jack)

The Old Forester Single Barrel that we used is 64.5% ABV. The idea was to provide a backbone to support the 38% ABV Becherovka, and it worked! Casey and I both really liked this cocktail.
