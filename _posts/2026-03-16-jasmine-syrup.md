## Ingredients

- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 heaping Tablespoon jasmine tea

## Directions

1. Place sugar and water in a pot over medium heat. Simmer until combined.
2. Turn off heat (but leave the pan on the burner), add the jasmine tea, stir briefly, and cover to steep.
3. Allow to steep (covered) for 15 minutes, or until your desired flavor is reached.
