## Ingredients

- 1 bag (14.4 oz) frozen pearl onions
- 1 Tablespoon butter
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- Salt and pepper

## Directions

1. Thaw pearl onions.
2. SautÃ© onions in butter until golden brown. Deglaze with a little water.
3. Add heavy cream, and reduce until you have a smooth consistency. Season to taste..
